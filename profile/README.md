## 🖌 Overview  
Obsidian Advanced is a local-first knowledge base that lets you build a second brain from plain markdown files. Every note you write can link to any other note, creating a personal web of connected ideas that grows more valuable over time.

Your notes live as plain text files in a folder on your device. No proprietary format, no vendor lock-in, no internet required. The data is yours in the most literal sense.

Bidirectional links let you navigate your knowledge in any direction. The graph view visualizes every connection, revealing relationships between ideas you might not have noticed otherwise.

A rich plugin ecosystem extends Obsidian Advanced with hundreds of community-built tools — from task managers and calendars to spaced repetition and canvas boards.

For thinkers, researchers, writers, and knowledge workers who want a thinking environment that truly fits their mind, Obsidian Advanced is the app that grows with you.

## 🚀 Why Choose Obsidian Advanced?

Obsidian Advanced represents the perfect balance between powerful functionality and elegant simplicity. Built specifically for Apple Silicon, this application takes full advantage of cutting-edge technologies including Metal graphics acceleration, Grand Central Dispatch for multi-threading, and native components that ensure buttery-smooth performance even on older hardware.

The application has been meticulously optimized for both Intel-based systems and the latest Apple Silicon chips, including M1, M2, M3, and M4 processors. Whether you are running older hardware or the latest devices, you will experience the same level of responsiveness and efficiency.

What truly sets Obsidian Advanced apart is its commitment to user privacy and data security. All processing happens locally on your device, with no data ever sent to external servers. Your documents and work remain completely private and under your control.

Regular updates bring not just bug fixes, but meaningful feature enhancements based on real user feedback. The development team actively listens to the community and implements requested improvements, ensuring the software evolves alongside your needs.

The application seamlessly integrates with your existing workflow, supporting drag-and-drop functionality, Quick Look previews, Spotlight searching, and even Shortcuts automation for power users.
